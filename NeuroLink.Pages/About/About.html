NeuroDrive: Revolutionizing Autonomous Mobility NeuroDrive is an intelligent, autonomous toy car powered by Embedded Systems and Artificial Intelligence, designed to demonstrate the integration of advanced technologies into resource-constrained platforms. At its core, the system utilizes a Raspberry Pi to seamlessly combine facial recognition , voice command recognition, traffic sign detection, object avoidance, and line-following functionalities. The YOLOv8n model is employed for real-time traffic sign recognition, while lightweight JavaScript APIs enable responsive facial and voice interactions. Ultrasonic and infrared sensors ensure safe navigation by detecting obstacles and following predefined paths. The system also features environmental monitoring capabilities, including temperature, humidity, and gas detection, showcased through the NeuroLink web interface. Designed for efficiency and user-friendliness, NeuroDrive highlights the potential of embedding AI in low-power systems, offering insights into the development of smart, autonomous technologies. Despite challenges like hardware limitations and environmental sensitivity, the project underscores the feasibility of integrating AI-driven functionalities into everyday applications.
